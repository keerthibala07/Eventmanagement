Crescita is a national-level symposium conducted every year by the Computer Science and Engineering department of Kongu Engineering College. Crescita, a great platform to showcase your innovative ideas and enhance your technical skills, features both technical and fun events. Crescita consistently excels in identifying the best in individuals and rewarding them accordingly. We warmly welcome you all to challenge yourselves in various areas.
